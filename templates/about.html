About EHR System

Our Mission

The Electronic Health Record (EHR) System is designed to streamline patient data management for healthcare professionals. Our goal is to provide a simple, secure, and efficient solution for doctors to maintain comprehensive patient records.

Features
  • Secure doctor registration and login
  • Complete patient record management
  • Medical history tracking
  • Report image uploads
  • Private data isolation per doctor
